OPTION 2

Pixel Art / Retro

Nostalgic & Charming

Everything rendered on a chunky pixel grid like a Game Boy game. The chicken becomes an animated 16x16 pixel bird with wing-flap frames. Pipes get pixel shading. Ground becomes a tiled repeating pattern. Restricted 8-16 color palette per level.

KEY CHANGES

Small offscreen canvas, scaled up crisp

Chicken gets 2-4 frame flap animation

Pipes gain pixel highlight/shadow

Ground becomes tiled pixel texture

EFFORT: MEDIUM

Need to design pixel art shapes, add animation frames

BEST FOR

Retro appeal, personality, clear game-feel

INSPIRATION

Original Flappy Bird, Celeste, Shovel Knight
